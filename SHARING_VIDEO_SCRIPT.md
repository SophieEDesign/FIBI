# How to Share to FiBi - Video Script

## Video Overview
A simple, clear tutorial showing users how to share content from their phone to FiBi. Target length: 30-60 seconds.

---

## Script

### Opening (0-5 seconds)
**Visual:** Show phone home screen with FiBi app icon
**Narration (optional):** "Save places from TikTok, Instagram, and more directly to FiBi"

### Step 1: Find Content (5-10 seconds)
**Visual:** 
- Open TikTok/Instagram app
- Scroll to find a post/video about a place (restaurant, destination, etc.)
- Tap on the post to view it

**Narration (optional):** "Find a post or video you want to save"

### Step 2: Tap Share (10-15 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Show the share button on the post (arrow icon or "Share" button)
- Tap the share button
- Share sheet opens showing various apps

**Narration (optional):** "Tap the share button"

### Step 3: Select FiBi (15-25 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Scroll through the share sheet
- Highlight/tap on the FiBi app icon
- Show FiBi icon clearly visible in the share options

**Narration (optional):** "Select FiBi from your share options"

### Step 4: Auto-Preview (25-35 seconds)
**Visual:**
- FiBi app opens automatically
- Show the Add Item form with:
  - URL pre-filled
  - Visual preview automatically loaded
  - Title and description pulled through
- Optionally show user adding a screenshot or location

**Narration (optional):** "FiBi automatically pulls through a preview. Add details if you want, then save!"

### Closing (35-40 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Show the saved item in the FiBi home grid
- Quick pan across a few saved items

**Narration (optional):** "That's it! Your place is saved."

**Text overlay:** "Install FiBi to start saving places"

## Video Specifications

### Recommended Format
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical/portrait) for mobile viewing
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 or 720x1280
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds
- **Style:** Clean, simple, easy to follow

### Recording Tips
1. Use screen recording on actual phone (not simulator)
2. Use real apps (TikTok, Instagram) for authenticity
3. Keep transitions smooth and not too fast
4. Add subtle zoom/highlight on important buttons
5. Use clear, readable text overlays for step numbers

### Text Overlays (Optional)
- Step numbers (1, 2, 3, 4)
- Brief instructions ("Tap Share", "Select FiBi")
- Key features ("Auto-preview", "No copy-paste needed")

## Quick Reference: Step-by-Step Text Version

1. **Open** TikTok, Instagram, or any app
2. **Find** a post/video about a place you want to save
3. **Tap** the Share button (arrow icon or Share)
4. **Select** FiBi from the share options
5. **Review** the auto-filled preview in FiBi
6. **Save** your place!
